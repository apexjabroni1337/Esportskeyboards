CS2 Pro Settings & Tips
Counter-Strike 2 professionals prioritize two core keyboard features: rapid trigger capability and ultra-low actuation distance. These enable pixel-perfect counter-strafing and split-second weapon switching crucial in competitive rounds. Most pros operate their rapid trigger between 0.2mm and 2.5mm for maximum responsiveness while maintaining consistency during extended tournaments.
"Master your actuation distance first. Most pros test 0.5mm-1.5mm range before settling. Once locked in, muscle memory builds quickly." — Interview with professional CS2 player

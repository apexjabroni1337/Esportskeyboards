About the Wooting 60HE+

The Wooting 60HE+ is the most trusted keyboard in professional esports. Engineered for competitive advantage, it features Lekker Hall Effect switches with adjustable rapid trigger capability, allowing pro players to fine-tune their input response for their specific game.

With 8000Hz polling rate (0.125ms response), the 60HE+ delivers zero-latency input processing. The compact 60% layout maximizes desk space while providing full functionality with hot-swappable switches, giving you complete customization freedom.

Used by world champions across CS2, Valorant, and esports, the Wooting 60HE+ has become the de facto standard for professional tournament play. Every pro keystroke is captured with precision.

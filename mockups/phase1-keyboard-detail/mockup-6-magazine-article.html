Wooting 60HE+: The Keyboard That Changed Professional Esports

By Editorial Team | February 2026 | 12 min read
[Editorial Product Photography]

The keyboard has always been the unsung hero of professional gaming. While monitors get the marketing dollars and headsets get the airtime, it's the humble keyboard that bears the weight of every keystroke, every clutch decision, and every millisecond that separates champions from contenders. The Wooting 60HE+ has fundamentally changed this narrative.

Launched in 2023, the Wooting 60HE+ arrived at a critical juncture in esports hardware evolution. Professional gamers had been pushing the boundaries of what was possible with conventional keyboard switches for years. They wanted faster response times, adjustable actuation points, and the ability to customize their input experience. The Wooting 60HE+ answered every single one of these demands with 8000Hz polling rate technology and groundbreaking Hall Effect switches.

"I've tried every keyboard on the market. The Wooting 60HE+ isn't just better—it's in a different category entirely. It's the first keyboard that actually listens to what pro players have been asking for."

What makes the 60HE+ revolutionary isn't any single feature, but rather how thoughtfully these features work together. The Lekker Hall Effect switches eliminate the mechanical inconsistencies that plague traditional Cherry MX switches. With adjustable rapid trigger and adjustable actuation, players can fine-tune their keyboard to match their unique playstyle and the specific demands of their game.

The adoption rate tells the real story. Within months of release, the Wooting 60HE+ was spotted in every major CS:GO tournament. Professional players like s1mple, TenZ, ZywOo, donk, and NiKo publicly endorsed the keyboard, not because of sponsorship deals, but because it genuinely made them better at their craft. Today, at 15.2% pro usage rate, it's the most-used keyboard in professional esports.

The 60% compact layout provides mobility without sacrifice—essential for tournament players who might need to relocate their setup between matches. The PBT doubleshot keycaps ensure durability through years of intensive competitive play, while the hot-swappable design gives players complete control over their switch selection.

"When you're competing at the highest level, every advantage matters. The Wooting 60HE+ isn't just a keyboard; it's a competitive advantage."

At $175, the Wooting 60HE+ sits in the premium segment, but professional players and serious competitors consider it a non-negotiable investment. The keyboard doesn't just perform—it delivers a 9.4 out of 10 rating from the community, marking it as one of the highest-rated keyboards ever created. The USB-C wired connection ensures zero latency concerns in critical tournament moments.

The Wooting 60HE+ represents the maturation of esports hardware. It's no longer about incremental improvements; it's about fundamental innovation that changes how professional players approach their craft. Whether you're competing at the highest levels or simply want to experience the keyboard that changed professional esports, the Wooting 60HE+ is the unmissable choice.
